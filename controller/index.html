Slide Mobile Manager

Control your presentation slides from your phone while your computer stays on any network.

  • Agent app on your PC listens for Next/Prev commands.
  • Secure backend routes commands between your phone and PC.
  • Controller web page runs on your phoneno install required.
Open controller Download Windows agent

1) Install and run the Agent on your presentation computer.
2) Scan the QR code in the Agent window or tap Open controller on your phone.
3) Enter the short code and tap Next/Prev.

How it works

  • 1 Agent on your PC connects to the backend and receives a unique session code.
  • 2 Your phone opens the controller page and joins using that code.
  • 3 Button presses on the phone travel through the backend to your Agent, which presses the keyboard arrows for you.

For best results, keep your slides app (PowerPoint, Keynote, etc.) in full-screen and focused on the presentation computer.
